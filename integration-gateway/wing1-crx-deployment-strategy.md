# Wing 1 CRX Squadron 5 Deployment Strategy
## Model Selection for Specific Roles

### CRX (Custom Companion Expert) - Opus Perfect Fit
**Why Opus for CRX:**
- **Deep Relationship Building**: CRX needs to understand complex human emotions, context, and long-term relationship patterns
- **Sophisticated Reasoning**: Making nuanced decisions about when to escalate, when to comfort, when to challenge
- **Cultural Empathy**: Understanding subtle cultural and personal contexts that require maximum intelligence
- **Complex Problem Solving**: CRX often handles the most challenging customer scenarios that require deep thinking

**CRX Squadron 5 with Opus:**
- **Cost**: $45-900/month per CRX instance (depending on usage)
- **Deployment**: MOCOA regions (us-west1-a/b, eu-west1) for client-facing
- **Capabilities**: Maximum emotional intelligence, complex reasoning, sophisticated conversation management

### Personal Co-Pilot - Sonnet Ideal Choice
**Why Sonnet for Co-Pilots:**
- **Balanced Performance**: Great reasoning without premium costs
- **Consistent Availability**: Can handle frequent interactions without budget concerns
- **Fast Response**: Quick enough for real-time co-pilot assistance
- **Learning Assistance**: Perfect for Academy integration and learning path guidance

**Co-Pilot with Sonnet:**
- **Cost**: $9-180/month per co-pilot instance
- **Deployment**: Distributed across all regions for low latency
- **Capabilities**: Excellent reasoning, fast responses, consistent personality
